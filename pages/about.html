A Word from CEO

As the CEO of SAM Life Savers, I am honored to lead an organization dedicated to uplifting the most vulnerable members of our society. Our mission is to provide hope, support, and a better future for orphans, the elderly, transgender individuals, and those in dire need.

At SAM Life Savers, we believe that kindness and collective action can transform lives. Through our initiatives in welfare, food distribution, healthcare, and disaster management, we strive to make a lasting impact. Every life we touch is a step toward a more compassionate and united world.

I invite you to join us in this journey of change through volunteering or spreading awareness & become our #Humanitarian. Together, we can be the reason someone smiles today.

We Rise By Lifting Others

Shavez Adalat

CEO – SAM Life Savers
